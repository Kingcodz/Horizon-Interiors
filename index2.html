Our Journey

Founded in 2015 by award-winning designer Olivia Richardson, Horizon Interior Designs began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create spaces that inspire, nurture, and elevate the human experience.

What started as a boutique studio in New York has grown into a renowned design collective, bringing together talented designers, architects, and artisans from around the world. Our portfolio spans residential sanctuaries, commercial landmarks, and hospitality destinations across the globe.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as industry leaders, having received multiple awards for our innovative designs and sustainable practices. Our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Values & Philosophy

Our design philosophy is built on four core principles that guide every project we undertake.

Client-Centric Design

Every design decision is made with our clients' needs, preferences, and lifestyle in mind. Your story is at the heart of our creative process.

Sustainable Innovation

We believe in creating spaces that are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible, using sustainable materials and practices.

Attention to Detail

From the grandest architectural elements to the smallest decorative touches, we ensure every detail contributes to the overall vision.

Collaborative Spirit

We foster strong partnerships with clients, architects, craftsmen, and artists to bring the finest expertise to each project.
